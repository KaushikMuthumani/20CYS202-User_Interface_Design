AEEE
Amrita engineering entrance exam

AEEE is a prestigious, pan-India entrance exam conducted for engineering aspirants to get admission into Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham. This objective-type exam is based on three core subjects Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. Exams are conducted offline in various centers across the country, and qualified students will be guided through the admission process.
