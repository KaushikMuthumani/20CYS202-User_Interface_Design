Workout Plans

Beginner Workout Plan

05:00

A simple workout plan for beginners to get started on their fitness journey.

  • Warm-up: 5 minutes of light cardio
  • Exercise 1: Push-ups - 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Exercise 2: Bodyweight Squats - 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Exercise 3: Plank - 3 sets of 30 seconds

Intermediate Workout Plan

10:00

Take your fitness to the next level with this intermediate workout plan.

  • Warm-up: 10 minutes of light cardio
  • Exercise 1: Dumbbell Bench Press - 4 sets of 12 reps
  • Exercise 2: Lunges - 4 sets of 15 reps per leg
  • Exercise 3: Plank with Leg Lifts - 4 sets of 30 seconds

Advanced Workout Plan

15:00

Challenge yourself with this advanced workout plan designed for experienced fitness enthusiasts.

  • Warm-up: 15 minutes of dynamic stretching
  • Exercise 1: Deadlifts - 5 sets of 5 reps
  • Exercise 2: Pull-ups - 5 sets to failure
  • Exercise 3: Plank to Pike - 5 sets of 20 seconds

Workout Plan for Overweight Individuals

10:00

A tailored workout plan for individuals looking to lose weight and improve fitness.

  • Warm-up: 10 minutes of brisk walking
  • Exercise 1: Bodyweight Squats - 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Exercise 2: Walking Lunges - 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
  • Exercise 3: Modified Push-ups - 3 sets of 8 reps

Workout Plan for Underweight Individuals

05:00

A workout plan designed to help underweight individuals build muscle and gain strength.

  • Warm-up: 5 minutes of light cardio
  • Exercise 1: Compound Exercises - 4 sets of 8-10 reps (e.g., Squats, Deadlifts)
  • Exercise 2: Dumbbell Rows - 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Exercise 3: Plank - 3 sets of 30 seconds
